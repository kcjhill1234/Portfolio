About Me

I spent 27 years in the grocery retail industry and had an amazing career. I was in management for 19 of the 27 years and the most rewarding aspect was being apart of the lives I affected. I am currently enrolled and on my way to getting a bachelors degree. My top 2 loves are my kids and basketball, which I play weekly .I’m also a certified master diver with about 70 logged dives. I’ve been to Hawaii, Honduras, and Curacao.
